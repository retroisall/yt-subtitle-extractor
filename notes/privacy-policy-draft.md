# Privacy Policy — YouTube Learning Bar

**Last updated: April 27, 2026**

## Overview

YouTube Learning Bar ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that enhances your YouTube experience with subtitle display, translation, vocabulary tracking, and community subtitle features.

## Data We Collect

### Data stored locally on your device
- Extension settings and preferences (via `chrome.storage.local`)
- Personal vocabulary / word book entries
- Custom subtitle files you create

### Data stored in our cloud (Firebase / Firestore)
When you sign in with your Google account:
- Your Google account email address and display name (for authentication)
- Your vocabulary / word book (synced across devices)
- Community subtitle files you choose to upload (these become publicly accessible by other users)
- Editor permission status (whether you have permission to use the subtitle editor)

### Data we do NOT collect
- Your YouTube watch history
- Subtitle content of videos you watch (processed locally only)
- Browsing history outside of YouTube
- Any payment information

## How We Use Your Data

- **Authentication**: Your Google account is used to identify you for cloud sync and community features.
- **Vocabulary sync**: Your word book is stored in Firestore to enable cross-device access.
- **Community subtitles**: Subtitles you upload are stored publicly under your Google account identifier. Other users can view and use them.
- **Analytics**: We do not collect any analytics or usage data.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension uses the following external services:
- **Firebase / Firestore** (Google): Cloud storage for vocabulary and community subtitles. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google Translate API**: Real-time translation of subtitle text. No data is stored.
- **Free Dictionary API** (api.dictionaryapi.dev): Word definitions. No personal data is sent.
- **Datamuse API**: Word suggestions. No personal data is sent.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by the third-party services listed above to operate the Extension.

Community subtitles you upload are publicly visible to all users of the Extension.

## Data Retention

- Local data (settings, word book cache) remains on your device until you uninstall the Extension or clear Chrome storage.
- Cloud data (vocabulary, community subtitles) remains until you request deletion.

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of your cloud data by contacting us. To delete your data:
1. Sign in to the Extension
2. Your vocabulary data can be deleted from the Vocabulary Dashboard
3. Community subtitles you uploaded can be removed from the subtitle browser
